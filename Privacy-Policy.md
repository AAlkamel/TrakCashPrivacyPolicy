# Privacy Policy – TrakCash

**Last Updated:** April 19, 2026

## 1. Introduction

TrakCash values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

TrakCash is designed with a privacy-first approach, meaning most of your sensitive information is stored locally on your device.

TrakCash does not require user accounts or personal authentication.

By using TrakCash, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information Collection & Privacy by Design

### 2.1 Personal & Financial Data (Local Only)

TrakCash does not collect, store, or transmit your financial data to external servers.

All the following information is stored locally on your device:

* Transaction details (amounts, dates, categories, descriptions)
* Budget limits and financial goals
* Recurring transactions
* User preferences (currency, language, theme settings)

We do not access or process your data remotely.

If your device is lost or the app is uninstalled without backup, this data cannot be recovered.

### 2.2 Anonymized Technical Data

To improve app stability and performance, we may collect limited anonymized technical data, including:

* Device type and operating system version
* App interaction events (screen views, feature usage, button clicks)
* Crash reports and performance diagnostics

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security

* All data is stored locally in a secure application directory on your device.
* No cloud synchronization is used by default.
* No user accounts or login system is required.
* We use platform-native security mechanisms and secure application storage practices to protect locally stored data.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

TrakCash uses limited third-party services for app performance and stability:

* Google Play Services (app distribution and updates)
* Firebase Analytics
* Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect anonymized data such as:

* Screen views and feature interactions
* Crash logs and device performance metrics
* Non-personal technical identifiers

❌ No financial data or transaction details are collected or shared with any third party.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data.

We only share anonymized and aggregated technical data for:

* App performance monitoring
* Crash detection and bug fixing
* Improving user experience

This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

---

## 6. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliant)

You have the following rights:

* **Right to Access:** View your data inside the app
* **Right to Portability:** Export your data (CSV/JSON if available)
* **Right to Correction:** Modify your data at any time inside the app
* **Right to Deletion:** Delete your data via:

  * In-app delete feature

Where applicable, we comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations.

---

## 7. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your data at any time by:

* Using the in-app deletion feature
* Uninstalling the application

Where available, in-app deletion permanently removes all locally stored data.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

TrakCash is not directed at children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and we do not target minors.

If we become aware that such data exists, it will be deleted immediately.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app improvements or legal requirements.

Any updates will be posted within the app or on our website.

The "Last Updated" date will always reflect the latest version.
